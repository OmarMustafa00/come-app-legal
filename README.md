# Account and Data Deletion Instructions for COME App

This section outlines the data deletion policy for the **COME App**, developed by **Quantum Crest**. We respect your privacy and provide a straightforward process to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data.

### How to Request Account Deletion
If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:
1. Send an email to **0omar.mustafa0@gmail.com** from the email address associated with your COME App account.
2. Use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**.
3. In the body of the email, state clearly that you want your account and all related data to be permanently deleted.

### Types of Data Deleted
Upon receiving and verifying your request, we will permanently delete the following data from our Firebase database and storage within 30 days:
* **Personal Profile Data:** Your name and registered email address.
* **User-Generated Content:** Any custom folders, vocabulary phrases, and emoji images you have uploaded or created.
* **Communication Data:** Your chat history, sent messages, and linked symbols.

### Data Retained and Retention Period
* **No personal data is retained.** Once the deletion process is complete, all your personal identifying information and user-generated content are permanently erased from our active servers. 
* *Note:* Secure backup copies of the database may retain encrypted fragments of data for an additional 60 days strictly for disaster recovery purposes, after which they are automatically overwritten and destroyed.
